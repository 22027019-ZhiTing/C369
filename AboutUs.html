About Us

The ZT Bank safeguards the interests of customers and comply with regulations by the Monetary Authority of Singapore.

Our goal is to protect consumers from frauds and to also prevent money laundering.

Financial services we offer: Local and international transfer to any mobile/identification or account numbers.



We allow for Singpass Sign up or Log in to prevent fraud cases.

We also require user authentication of pin numbers during transactions to mitigate Money Laundering.



If you see any suspicious activities in your account or you are suspecting anything, feel free to report it!
